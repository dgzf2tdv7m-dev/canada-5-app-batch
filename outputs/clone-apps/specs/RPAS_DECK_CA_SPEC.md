# RPAS Deck CA - SwiftUI iOS 26+ Paid App Spec

## Product Positioning

RPAS Deck CA is a premium offline-first field deck for Canadian drone pilots who need fast pre-flight decisions, bilingual checklists, and clean mission documentation. The app supports Transport Canada RPAS planning workflows for micro, small, and emerging complex operation categories, but it does not submit authorizations or replace official sources such as Transport Canada, NAV CANADA, the Drone Management Portal, or Canadian Aviation Regulations.

Primary buyers: Canadian drone pilots, small inspection teams, real estate/media operators, municipal responders, flight reviewers, and RPAS students who want a polished paid utility with no ads or accounts.

Reference sources checked June 17, 2026:
- Transport Canada Drone Safety: https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/drone-safety
- Transport Canada 2025 RPAS regulation changes: https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/drone-safety/2025-summary-changes-canada-drone-regulations
- Transport Canada Advanced Operations: https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/drone-safety/learn-rules-you-fly-your-drone/drone-operation-categories-pilot-certificates/advanced-operations

- Justice Laws, Canadian Aviation Regulations Part IX: https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-96-433/

## Core Screens And Features

1. Today Deck
   - Current readiness panel: pilot, aircraft, weather, airspace, mission, battery, documents.
   - One-tap "New Brief" and "Continue Last Mission".
   - Status chips: Ready, Review, Blocked, Verify Official Source.

2. Operation Classifier
   - Inputs: drone takeoff weight, VLOS/BVLOS/EVLOS, airspace type, distance from people, over people, controlled airspace, event/emergency constraints, indoor/sheltered context.
   - Output: likely planning path such as Micro, Basic, Advanced, Sheltered, EVLOS, Level 1 Complex, or SFOC/official review.
   - Shows required credentials, aircraft declarations, authorization prompts, and "verify before flight" notes.

3. Mission Brief
   - Structured sections: site, objective, crew, aircraft, airspace, weather, hazards, privacy, emergency plan, client notes.
   - Bilingual checklist labels.
   - PDF export for client/job folders.

4. Airspace And Source Checklist
   - Not a live aeronautical map unless licensed.
   - Provides official-source launch buttons and manual confirmation rows for NAV Drone, NOTAMs, CFS/supplement, local restrictions, parks, municipal bylaws, wildfire/emergency limits, and property permission.

5. Aircraft Vault
   - Cards for drones, batteries, payloads, firmware, registration marks, maintenance notes, payload weight presets.
   - Weight threshold warnings around 250 g, 25 kg, and 150 kg.

6. Pilot Wallet
   - Certificate type, exam date, flight review date, recurrent training notes, insurance, client-required docs.
   - Local reminders only; no cloud account required.

7. Field Checklists
   - Templates: Micro recreational, Basic VLOS, Advanced VLOS, controlled airspace, near people, sheltered, EVLOS prep, cold weather, night/twilight, emergency response support.
   - Every checklist supports duplicate, customize, lock, export, and reset.

8. Logbook
   - Flight records with aircraft, pilot, location, duration, batteries, conditions, incidents, maintenance flags.
   - CSV and PDF export.

9. Reference Cards
   - Offline summaries for common decision points.
   - Each card has "Last reviewed" date and source link.

10. Settings
   - Language: System, English, French.
   - Units: metric default, optional mixed aviation display.
   - Privacy mode hides client names and exact coordinates in screenshots.

## Privacy Page Copy

RPAS Deck CA is designed as a private paid utility. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not sell personal information.

Data you enter, including aircraft details, pilot credentials, mission briefs, checklists, locations, client notes, and logbook entries, is stored on your device unless you choose to export or share it using iOS system features. If iCloud sync is added in a future version, it should be opt-in and clearly disclosed before use.

The app may open official Transport Canada, NAV CANADA, weather, or mapping websites in your browser. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

RPAS Deck CA may collect anonymous crash diagnostics only if the user has enabled Apple system diagnostics. No third-party advertising SDKs, broker SDKs, or cross-app tracking should be included.

Privacy summary:
- Account required: No
- Ads or tracking: No
- Data sold: No
- Local mission records: Yes
- Optional exports: User controlled
- Precise location: Only if the user grants permission for mission/site capture

## Implementation Notes

- Minimum platform: iOS 26.0, iPadOS 26.0.
- Architecture: SwiftUI, SwiftData local storage, Observation, AppIntents for quick "New Brief" and "Log Flight".
- No backend required for v1.
- Store source reference cards as versioned JSON bundled with app; show review date in UI.
- Use `Locale` and `.stringsdict` for English/French; avoid hardcoded strings in views.
- Export via PDFKit/SwiftUI renderer and CSV document exporter.
- Treat location permission as optional. Manual site entry must work without location access.
- Regulatory content must be editable via app update and clearly source-labeled.
